Let's Play Chess!

Chess is a strategy game played by two people. The goal is to capture the enemy king. Tactics and memorizing past games is a good way to achieve victory!

Pawn
How to play

Moves one square vertically at a time. Captures diagonally on both sides. Can move two squares on its first move. This piece is the only one that cannot go back. After reaching the last rank, it can promote to any chess piece except a king.

1 point
Knight
How to play

A powerful piece that "jumps". Moves in an L-shape fashion that is composed of two squares and another square, e.g. two squares up, then one square left, or, two squares left, then one square down.

3 point
Bishop
How to play

Moves diagonally. You have a light squared and a dark squared bishop. Best played by sniping through diagonals that is usually used to pin something.

3 point
